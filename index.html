About Joe

Joe began coding in the late 90's on GeoCities and with MySpace customizations. He later went on to do design work for his first employer, but it wasn't his primary function. After designing his own website as a hobby, Joe knew he wanted to do web coding for a living, but nobody was hiring full-time web coders at the time. Joe also didn't know how to go into business for himself, so he left coding behind as a passion.

Fast forward about twenty years, and Joe's at a crossroads. He wants a new career, but doesn't know how. When his friend suggested they both join a coding bootcamp, Joe jumped at the chance to start again!
